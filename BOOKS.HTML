Digital Marketing All - In - One
For Dummies Paperback – 1 December 2020 by Stephanie Diamond (Author)

Written By: Ryan Deiss and Russ Henneberry Release Date: Dec 27, 2016 Pages: 328 Price: $20.18 Many wish to have a magic book with ‘for dummies’ suffixed in its title to have an easy guide on the subject that interests them. Luckily for people with interest in digital marketing, there is already a bestseller book for you in this department. Digital Marketing for Dummies
is a bare-boned approach of teaching digital marketing for readers, offering explanations to some of its most basic concepts in the simplest way possible before ultimately heading into the more advanced subject matter. What is astonishing about
this book is despite its raw approach to the topic, how relevant the book remains in the year 2022. It talks about everything from the current best SEO practices to effectively using content marketing to gain traction. This book covers every inch of the digital marketing universe in the most comprehensive way possible.
The book aims to teach novice marketers how to come up with a digital marketing plan that works for their business. It is well thought out, and most importantly very easy to read. This book is definitely worth a check.
Suggested Readers Everyone, Small and Business Owners, Digital Marketing Enthusiasts
